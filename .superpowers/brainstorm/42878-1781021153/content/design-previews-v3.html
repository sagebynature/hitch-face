The Proposed Hybrid Design (V3):

This layout integrates the strengths of both designs while solving the alignment bugs:

  • BMO Remains Stable: Opening the side console drawer no longer pushes BMO's casing to the left. BMO stays centered in the transparent Electron window, and the console slides out to the right from behind BMO using CSS transform: scaleX().
  • Cartridge Stays Aligned: The game cartridge resides in BMO's local container, so its positioning is completely unaffected by the drawer opening/closing.
  • Screen HUD + Real-time Scrolling Ticker: The green CRT screen shows the harness name in the top-left corner and a retro scrolling ticker tape at the bottom.
  • Red Button Toggle: Clicking BMO's physical Red Button toggles the Side Console Drawer. Clicking BMO's yellow button behaves normally (simple mechanical click style).

Click BMO's physical Red Button (highlighted with a dashed pink outline) or the trigger buttons at the top of the container to test the toggle!
